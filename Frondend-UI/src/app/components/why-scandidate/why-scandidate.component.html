Why SCANDIDATE?

We do a Candid Scan on every Candidate you want to hire. We exist and operate purely with the support, collaboration and partnership of Human Resources professionals across different sectors. And, we therefore can provide pure, unadulterated support, collaboration and partnership to Human Resources professionals across different sectors. Yes, we are the bridge of information, from one HRD to another, about each person leaving one company and joining another. Scandidate is a candid career scan for each experienced professional.

Why should you give your ex-employees' information?

You anyway do it - every time an ex-employee joins a new organization, someone from your management team or HR is spending considerable effort in helping the new organization complete their background checks. And, you may not be doing you good performers justice.

Instead, let SCANDIDATE be your journal to record very insightful details about your ex-employees, AT the time of exit! You will lend incredible value to the careers of your ex-employees!
